Haukai Restaurant Privacy Policy

What data we collect:

When you visit our website we may collect information about your:

  • location
  • computer or network
  • interactions with us

Additionally, when you request to make a booking with us we also ask you for your:

  • name
  • contact information

Why we collect your data:

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • keep in contact with you about your bookings.
  • gather analytical information about how the website is used to allow us to improve our services and the user experience

How we keep your data safe:

  • We keep your information safe by storing it in password protected systems and only allowing staff who need it, to access it for their work perposes .
  • We do not share your data with any third parties.

How we dispose of your data:

Once your the date of your booking has passed, we delete your name and contact details from our records. We may keep other data for up to 3 years.

Correcting your data:

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.
